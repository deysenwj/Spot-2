The Iconic Jembatan Kaca

Jembatan Kaca Ngalam Indonesia di Kota Malang diresmikan pada 9 Oktober 2017 sebagai jembatan kaca pertama di Malang. Jembatan ini dibangun di atas Sungai Brantas untuk menghubungkan Kampung Warna-Warni Jodipan dan Kampung Tridi yang sebelumnya terpisah oleh aliran sungai. Pembangunan dimulai pada Juni 2017 dan selesai pada akhir September 2017, lalu diresmikan oleh Wali Kota Malang saat itu, Moch Anton. Jembatan ini dirancang oleh mahasiswa Teknik Sipil Universitas Muhammadiyah Malang (UMM), Mahatma Aji dan Khoirul, dengan pendampingan Ir. Lukito Prasetyo, serta didukung oleh PT Indana Paint. Dengan panjang sekitar 25 meter, lebar 1,25–1,5 meter, dan ketinggian ±9,5 meter di atas Sungai Brantas, jembatan ini menggunakan konstruksi baja dan kaca tebal yang mampu menampung sekitar 50 orang. Kehadirannya bertujuan meningkatkan kunjungan wisata, memperkuat perekonomian warga, serta memberikan pengalaman unik menikmati pemandangan Kampung Tematik Jodipan dari ketinggian.

The Ngalam Indonesia Glass Bridge in Malang City was inaugurated on October 9, 2017, and is known as the first glass bridge in Malang. Built above the Brantas River, the bridge connects Kampung Warna-Warni Jodipan and Kampung Tridi, which were previously separated by the river. Construction began in June 2017 and was completed in late September 2017, before being officially inaugurated by the Mayor of Malang at the time, Moch Anton. The bridge was designed by Civil Engineering students from Universitas Muhammadiyah Malang (UMM), Mahatma Aji and Khoirul, under the supervision of Ir. Lukito Prasetyo, with support from PT Indana Paint. Measuring approximately 25 meters in length, 1.25–1.5 meters in width, and standing about 9.5 meters above the Brantas River, the bridge features a steel structure with thick glass panels and can accommodate around 50 people. Its construction aims to increase tourism visits, support local economic growth, and offer visitors a unique experience of viewing the Jodipan thematic village from above.
